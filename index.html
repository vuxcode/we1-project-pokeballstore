About Next race

The Käpplunda Sprint Race is a fast and exciting event held on the tarmac paths around Käpplunda in Skövde. The course is short, technical, and perfect for high-speed racing with tight turns and quick accelerations. Everything on wheels is allowed, and the race is run as individual time trials. Each participant races against the clock, making it a perfect format for both beginners and experienced riders.

Location

The event will be near the lake behind Käpplunda Skola
